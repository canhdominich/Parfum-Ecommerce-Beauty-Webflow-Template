You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers.

The longevity of our fragrances varies based on factors such as skin type, climate, and activity level. However, our carefully crafted scents are designed to have a lasting presence. On average, you can enjoy the captivating notes for 6 to 8 hours. To enhance the longevity, consider applying the fragrance to pulse points and using our complementary scented body products.
Our perfumes are dermatologically tested and created with high-quality ingredients. While they are generally suitable for sensitive skin, we recommend patch-testing on a small area first if you have specific allergies or concerns.
We are committed to sustainability by sourcing eco-friendly ingredients, using recyclable packaging, and minimizing our environmental footprint in production and distribution processes.
